About Us

Provider

Dr. Kurt Kristiansen, BC-PMHNP

Board Certified, Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner

Dr. Kurt Kristiansen initally got his start in medicin as a Nurse, graduating with his Bachelors in Nursing from Bellarmine University in Louisville, Kentucky, and working in both Neuro and Cardiac ICUs. To be able help patients further, he became a Nurse Practitioner, and gained his PMHNP from The University of Arizona in Tucson, Arizona.
Because Dr. Kurt has seen such a wide variety of patients as a Nurse and Nurse Practitioner, he is able to provide above and beyond care to his patients. He understands that mental health struggles can be just as serious as a physical injury, and he wants to help each and every one of his patients overcome their difficulties.
Dr. Kurt is dedicated to providing a safe, non-judgmental environment for each of his patients, cultivating a relationship where the patients feel secure to voice their feelings and concerns, regardless of their struggles. He is a compassionate, understanding and sensitive listener who is dedicated to finding solutions which will help patients live healthy, happy lives.
Dr. Kurt offers treaments for Mood Disorders, Psychosis, Schizophrenia, Trauma & PTSD, and Substance Use Disorders.

Psychiatric Services

Assess, Diagnose, Treat

Psychiatry is the branch of medicine focused on the diagnosis, treatment and prevention of mental, emotional and behavioral disorders. A psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner (PMHNP) is an advanced practice nurse who specializes in mental health, including substance use disorders. PMHNPs are qualified to assess, diagnose, and treat these disorders using psychotherapy, medications, and other interventions.

Telepsychiatry

Virtual Appointments

Telepsychiatry is the process of providing health care from a distance using technology like videoconferencing. Video-based telepsychiatry helps meet patients' needs for convenient, and readily-accessible behavioral health services. We deliver care through a secure, HIPAA-compliant platform. Robust evidence has demonstrated that telepsychiatry leads to improved clinical outcomes and high levels of patient satisfaction.
